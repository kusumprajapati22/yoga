About Us

Welcome to Siddhnathyogshala

At Samatvam Yoga Uchyate, we believe yoga is more than just a practice — it's a path to harmony, strength, and self-discovery. Rooted in tradition yet open to all, our space is a sanctuary where breath meets movement, and every posture brings you closer to balance — inside and out.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening a lifelong journey, our mission is to guide you toward physical vitality, mental clarity, and spiritual growth. Through mindful flows, meditative stillness, and a welcoming community, we aim to make wellness a way of life.
