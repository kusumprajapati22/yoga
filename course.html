Welcome to Samatvam Yoga Uchyate

We believe that true and lasting wellness goes beyond just physical fitness — it is a sacred balance of the body, mind, and soul. In today’s fast-paced world, we often seek health in isolation — focusing only on the physical while forgetting the emotional and spiritual aspects that sustain us from within. Our approach is different. We bring together the timeless wisdom of Yoga, Pooja, and Hawan, offering a powerful blend of movement, mindfulness, and sacred ritual.

Yoga strengthens and purifies the body, stills the mind, and cultivates presence. Pooja connects us with divine energy, invoking peace, devotion, and gratitude. And Hawan, the sacred fire ceremony, burns away negativity and infuses our environment with spiritual energy and protection. Together, these ancient practices create a holistic path toward healing, self-realization, and inner harmony.

Yoga at Home

Your home, your pace, your peace.
Our "Yoga at Home" program brings the experience of a personal studio right to your living room. With easy-to-follow routines and guided video sessions, you can enjoy the benefits of yoga in your own space — perfect for beginners and busy schedules alike.

Yoga at Studio

Step into serenity and support.
Join our in-studio sessions for a deeply immersive and community-driven practice. Our trained instructors offer personalized guidance to help you build strength, flexibility, and mental calm in a peaceful, distraction-free environment.

Online Yoga Class

Yoga that moves with you. Stream live or recorded yoga classes from any device, anywhere. Our online platform allows you to stay consistent with your practice, whether you're at home, traveling, or working odd hours. Flexible, accessible, and made for modern lifestyles.

Corporate Yoga

Healthier teams, happier workspaces. Our Corporate Yoga sessions are designed to reduce workplace stress, improve posture, and enhance focus. Whether online or on-site, these programs boost employee wellness and promote a calm, productive environment.

Pregnancy Yoga

Nurture your body and baby. Specially designed for expectant mothers, our Pregnancy Yoga classes focus on gentle stretches, breathing techniques, and relaxation. They help ease common pregnancy discomforts while preparing the body for labor and delivery in a safe and supportive space.

Kid's Yoga

Stretch. Smile. Shine. Our fun and interactive Kid’s Yoga classes help children develop physical strength, concentration, and emotional balance. With playful poses and calming techniques, we make wellness enjoyable and beneficial for growing minds and bodies.
